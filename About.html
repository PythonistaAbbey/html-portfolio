Personal Information

Hello! I’m Abiodun Musa Mustapha, a seasoned Molecular Biologist and Data Analyst based in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia. With a Master’s degree in Molecular Biology from Stockholm University and a minor in Data Analysis, my career spans over a decade of impactful work in microbiology and biomedical informatics. I have honed my skills in data mining, machine learning, and programming, making significant contributions to both academic research and industry applications.

My professional journey includes roles such as a Data Engineer at Meta (Facebook), where I organized and analyzed complex data sets, and a Biomedical Informatics Researcher at Stockholm University, where I applied AI and deep learning to predict disease spread and treatment responses. Currently, I teach Advanced Placement and IB Biology at Maarif Education, nurturing the next generation of scientists and bioinformaticians.
